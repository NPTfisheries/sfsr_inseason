\hypertarget{summary}{%
\subsection{Summary}\label{summary}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  Current PIT-tag expansions at the Krassel PIT-array (KRS; as of July 17th)\footnote{Scripts to reproduce these summaries are available at ``\url{https://github.com/NPTfisheries/sf_salmon_harvest.com}''.}:

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    655 natural-origin fish estimated from 118 observed tags
  \item
    560 integrated fish estimated from 80 tags with a ratio of approximately 7 fish/tag
  \end{itemize}
\item
  KRS was non-operational: 7/10, 7/11, 7/14, 7/15, and 7/16

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    Integrated and natural-origin PIT-tag passage during this period is estimated as \(\le\) 10 for each group.
  \item
    additional 70 integrated fish
  \item
    additional 56 natural-origin
  \end{itemize}
\item
  Ad-intact fish currently estimated above KRS: 1,341
\item
  The average passage date of \(\ge\) 75\% of the total return across KRS is July 15th (Figure \ref{fig:run-timing}).
\item
  End of season ad-intact total estimate: 1,788

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    NOR: (655 + 56)/.75 = 948
  \item
    Integrated: (560 + 70)/.75 = 840
  \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{assumptions}{%
\subsection{Assumptions}\label{assumptions}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  100\% detection efficiency at KRS

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    In 2021 and 2022 KRS detections efficiency was \$\textgreater\$95\%.
  \end{itemize}
\item
  Static interpolation rate of 2 tags when observations are typically decreasing.
\item
  Integrated fish observed at KRS is approximately 100 fish higher than the estimate at LGR.
\item
  Integrated tag ratio of 7 is slighter higher than actual rate for each release group.
\end{itemize}
