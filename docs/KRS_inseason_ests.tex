\hypertarget{summary}{%
\section{Summary}\label{summary}}

\begin{itemize}
\item
  Current PIT-tag expansions at the Krasel PIT-array (KRA; as of October 17th, 2023)\footnote{Scripts to reproduce these summaries are available at: ``\url{https://github.com/NPTfisheries/sf_salmon_harvest.com}''.}

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    656 natural-origin fish estimated from 118 observed tags and 18\% sampling/tagging rate (Tables 1 and 2)
  \item
    Using a 20\% tagging rate the estimate is 590.
  \end{itemize}
\item
  KRS was non-operational: 7/10, 7/11, 7/14, 7/15, and 7/16

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    Natural-origin PIT-tag passage during this period is estimated as \(\le\) 10 tags for an additional 56 natural-origin fish.
  \end{itemize}
\item
  The average passage date of \(\ge\) 75\% of the total return across KRS is July 15th (Figure \ref{fig:run-timing}).
\item
  End of season natural-origin estimate: 948

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    NOR: (656 + 56)/.75 = 949
  \end{itemize}
\item
  Current Lower Granite Estimate of McCall Integrated fish is 465.

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    Estimated at KRS after accounting for SbyC is 443 (95\% conversion)
  \end{itemize}
\item
  \textbf{Total ad-intact fish at KRS: 1,413}

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    949 (NOR) + 465 (Integrated) = 1,414
  \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

\hypertarget{assumptions}{%
\subsection{Assumptions}\label{assumptions}}

\begin{itemize}
\tightlist
\item
  100\% conversion of integrated fish to KRS.
\item
  100\% detection efficiency at KRS

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    In 2021 and 2022 KRS detections efficiency was \(\ge\) 95\%.
  \end{itemize}
\item
  Tagging rate at LGR of 18\%.

  \begin{itemize}
  \tightlist
  \item
    Only 4 out of 118 NOR tags were marked at LGR during the 29\% sampling rate, all others marked at 18\%.
  \end{itemize}
\item
  Static interpolation rate of 2 tags during the missed period at KRS when observations are typically decreasing.
\end{itemize}
